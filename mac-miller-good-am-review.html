Album

GO:OD AM

Mac Miller

2015

Top 3 Songs

  • ROS
  • Perfect Circle/Godspeed
  • Ascension
10

Genre: Hip Hop/Rap

Label: Warner Records

Review

Mac Miller's "GO:OD AM" stands as a testament to the late rapper's evolution both as an artist and as a person. Released in 2015, this album serves as a pivotal moment in Miller's career, marking a transition from the playful rhymes of his earlier work to a more introspective and mature style. And marks a special album in my eyes, as I discovered the album in a very difficult and transitional period in my life. So it has a special meaning and emotional connection with me.

The album kicks off with the track "Doors," immediately setting the tone with its dreamy production and Miller's laid-back flow. From there, the listener is taken on a journey through Miller's mind, exploring themes of self-discovery, addiction, and the pressures of fame.

Tracks like "Brand Name" and "Rush Hour" showcase Miller's lyrical prowess, with clever wordplay and introspective verses that invite listeners to delve deeper into the artist's psyche. Meanwhile, songs like "Perfect Circle / God Speed" and "Ascension" reveal a more vulnerable side of Miller as he grapples with his demons and strives for inner peace. Which serve as a foreshadowing to his untimely end. These are one of the best songs in the album personally for me.

One of the standout tracks on the album is "100 Grandkids," a catchy anthem that finds Miller reflecting on his journey thus far while looking towards the future with optimism. With its infectious hook and upbeat production, it's a track that's sure to get stuck in your head long after the album ends. And for me the best song on the album is ROS, which is a love ballad about the beauty and struggles of love.

Throughout "GO:OD AM," Miller's growth as an artist is evident not only in his lyrics but also in his production choices. Collaborations with artists like Thundercat, Miguel, and Little Dragon add depth and texture to the album, elevating it beyond a typical hip-hop record.

In conclusion, "GO:OD AM" is a testament to Mac Miller's talent and artistry. With its introspective lyrics, innovative production, and memorable hooks, it's an album that resonates long after the final track fades out. For fans of hip-hop looking for a thought-provoking and emotionally resonant listening experience, "GO:OD AM" is not to be missed.
